About

by2891 is my One-man Web Design Agency based in Chicago Illinois. My name is Jordan DeLaforgue, I was raised by my mother Lynda, director of Citizen Action IL, a non-profit she began in order to organize local communities and labor unions in their fight for workers and civil rights. She spent her entire life on the frontlines helping those who needed it hand the most. Now I look to continue her legacy in my own way with my skills in web design and development. I don't want to make websites for big companies and products, I want to help as many local businesses, educators and neighbors of mine as I can, wherever their web-based needs lie.

I specialize in web design and development with HTML, CSS and Javascript, and I dabble in just about everything else; from graphic design to game development. I approach all of my work with a "client's-client first" mentality. Before I even begin to work on a framework for a website or an app I research and find out exactly what it is I can do for you. Whether that is increasing your web presence and boosting your search results so people can find you easier, or designing you a custom website from the ground-up (retail, blog, portfolio, etc.), or something completely different. I aspire for each of my projects to be as unique as possible while still reflecting my client and their consumer's wants and needs.

I'm currently considering ALL new clients and project ideas so contact me through any of my social networking platforms or the contact form below and lets get your website made properly today! I work with efficiency and ease-of-use in mind at all times plus I try to make any project I take on fun and fitted to my clients needs.
